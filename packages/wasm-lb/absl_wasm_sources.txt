# Curated abseil source subset that compiles to Wasm under em++, ported
# verbatim from the lb_core feasibility study and re-verified against our
# pinned third_party/abseil-cpp submodule (lts_20260107 / 255c84d).
#
# Why a subset: pulling absl::flat_hash_map (used by Envoy's WRSQ/EDF LB
# base) cascades into the hashtablez sampler -> absl synchronization, time,
# and debugging. These 103 .cc compile that closure. Platform-specific
# waiters (futex/pthread/sem), elf_mem_image, vdso_support, and *_win.cc are
# intentionally absent: emscripten supplies its own single-threaded
# equivalents and abseil's own #ifdefs select them. low_level_hash.cc does
# not exist in this LTS (inlined elsewhere); do not add it.
#
# Paths are relative to $(ABSL) (third_party/abseil-cpp). Lines starting
# with '#' and blank lines are ignored by the Makefile.
#
# To extend: a link error naming an absl symbol means adding that module's
# .cc here (and, for wasm, checking it is not a platform-specific variant).
